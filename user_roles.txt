# User Roles and Permissions Documentation

This document describes the roles and permissions for the Restaurant ERP system.

## Roles Summary

1. **Admin**: Full system access, including staff management and system-wide visibility.
2. **Manager**: High administrative access, including dashboard analytics and customer management.
3. **Cashier**: POS (Point of Sale) operations, order management, and basic customer search.
4. **Waiter**: POS operations (orders, tables) and viewing menu.
5. **Chef**: Order management (viewing/updating order status) and inventory viewing.

---

## Detailed Permissions

### 1. Staff Management
- **Create/Update/Delete Staff**: [Admin Only]
- **Toggle Staff Status**: [Admin Only]
- **View Staff Details**: [Admin, Manager]

### 2. Dashboard & Analytics
- **Overview, Revenue, Top Items, Performance**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Real-time Metrics**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Inventory Alerts**: [Admin, Manager]

### 3. Customers
- **View All Customers**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Search Customers**: [Any logged-in staff]
- **Create/Update Customer**: [Any logged-in staff]
- **Delete Customer**: [Admin Only]

### 4. Reservations
- **View All Reservations**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Create Reservation**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Update Reservation Status**: [Admin, Manager]
- **Delete Reservation**: [Admin Only]
- **Guest Request**: [Public - No login required]

### 5. Orders & POS
- **View Orders**: [Any logged-in staff]
- **Create/Update/Delete Order**: [Any logged-in staff]
  *Note: Deletion is typically reserved for correcting errors; regular staff can perform this unless further restricted.*

### 6. Inventory & Suppliers
- **View Inventory/Suppliers**: [Any logged-in staff]
- **Create/Update Items/Suppliers**: [Any logged-in staff]
- **Manage Payments/Statements**: [Any logged-in staff]
  *Note: These routes are protected by authentication but currently don't have explicit role restrictions beyond that.*

---

## Technical Implementation Notes
- **Authentication**: JWT Bearer token required for all routes (except Guest Reservation Request).
- **Middleware**: Role enforcement is handled via `authorize('role1', 'role2')` in backend routes.
- **Default Role**: New staff accounts default to `cashier` if not specified.
- **Enumerated Roles**: `["admin", "manager", "cashier", "chef", "waiter"]`
